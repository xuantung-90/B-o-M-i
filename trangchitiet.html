Gia xng RON95 giam manh ngay 15/7



(LT) Lin B Cng Thng - Ti chnh va pht i thng bo cho bit, ti k iu hnh gi xng, du ngy 3/7, gi bn l xng du ng lot gim mnh. Trong , gi xng E5 RON92 c iu chnh gim 408 ng/lt; xng RON95-III gim 587 ng/lt; du diesel gim 5 ng/lt v du ha gim 30 ng/lt sau khi trch lp Qu Bnh n gi.

C th, tr mt hng du mazut, gi xng v cc mt hng du cn li cng i xung trong k iu hnh ngy hm nay (3/7). Theo , gi xng E5 RON92 gim 408 ng/lt, v mc 20.470 ng/lt; xng RON95-III l 21.428 ng/lt, gim 587 ng/lt. Ngoi ra, du diesel gim 5 ng, xung mc 18.169 ng/lt; du ha gim 30 ng/lt, gi mi l 17.926 ng/lt. Tuy vy, du mazut tng 36 ng/kg, ln mc 14.623 ng. Lin quan ti Qu bnh n, lin B quyt nh dng trch lp i vi tt c cc mt hng xng du, ng thi dng chi qu vi cc mt hng ny.

Mi y, trong bo co tnh hnh xng du gi Cc Qun l gi (B Ti chnh), B Cng Thng cho bit so vi u nm (k iu hnh ngy 3/1), gi bn l cc mt hng xng du (tr du mazut) gim 0,6-20,2%. So vi cng thi im nm 2022 (k iu hnh ngy 21/6/2022), gi bn l cc mt hng xng du gim 29,6-39,5%.

Vi kch bn gi thnh phm xng du th gii bnh qun 90 USD/thng, B Cng Thng tnh ton gi xng E5 RON 92 trong qu III c th mc 21.325 ng/lt; xng RON 95 l 21.597 ng/lt v du diesel l 18.115 ng/lt. Trng hp gi thnh phm th gii mc 98 USD/thng, gi du diesel d bo mc 19.415 ng/lt; xng E5 RON 92 l 22.657 ng/lt; cn gi xng RON 95 l 23.049 ng, cao hn hin ti gn 2.000 ng/lt.

Ti k iu hnh gi gn y nht (ngy 21/6), sau khi trch lp qu bnh n, gi xng E5 v RON95-III cng gi n nh. Tuy vy, du diesel tng 146 ng; du ha tng 133 ng/lt, trong khi du mazut gim 132 ng/kg. Theo Ngh nh 95/N-CP sa i, b sung mt s iu ca Ngh nh 83/2014 v kinh doanh xng, du c hiu lc t ngy 2/1/2022, gi xng du c iu chnh mi thng 3 ln vo cc ngy: 1, 11 v ngy 21 hng thng (tc l 10 ngy mt ln).
